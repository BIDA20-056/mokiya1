About Onalethata Mokiya

Onalethata is a young Kalanga Handsome man 'the Great Eagle', 'Dzhu'. What is 'Dzhu' this is a Kalanga word which is a direct translation of the word 'Eagle'

EDUCATION

Schooling life and school experiences of Onalethata Mokiya 'the Eagle','Dzhu'

The Eagle started his schooling life at a creche situated in the village of Nshakazhogwe (very small village back then....hahaha evryone kinda new each other, the notion "it takes a village to raise a child" was pretty much a norm back then) around 2006 - late 2007.Then he went on to genesis his primary education at Nshakazhogwe Primary School Early 2008, standard 1,taught by one of his distant relatives 'Mma Nkubu',he then ascended to grade 2 , he had gotten so many accolades in his standard 1 class.
